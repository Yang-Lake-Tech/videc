MARINE MAGNETIC

MARINE MAGNETIC:

Marine magnetic field survey is a measurement of the magnetic field strength of the Earth or its components over an area through suitable survey lines. Magnetic surveying is also used to locate magnetic materials in the search for buried objects such as shipwrecks, mines, etc.

Magnetic survey equipment in use:

  • Marine magnetic Sea Spy
  • SeaQuest 3-Axis Gradiometer

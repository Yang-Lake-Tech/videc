TAN PHU DONG WIND POWER PLANT
Tan Phu Dong Wind Power Plant is built with 36 Wind turbine generators (WTG), the total expected capacity of 150MW.
The project is built in the coastal area of Phu Tan commune, Tan Phu Dong district, Tien Giang province, about 58km southeast of My Tho city.​
VPL BEN TRE - WIND POWER PLANT
The Ben Tre VPL wind power plant project is located in Thua Duc and Thoi Thuan communes, Binh Dai district, Ben Tre province. The factory has a total capacity of 70MW. All wind turbines are installed in shallow waters near the shore.
BAC LIEU WIND POWER PLANT - PHASE 3
Bac Lieu Wind Power Plant - Phase 3 with an installed capacity of 141 MW with 47 wind turbine pillars, started in January 2018. Each wind turbine has a capacity of about 2.5 - 3.5 MW. The expected annual electricity output is 373 million kWh.
BINH DAI WIND POWER PLANT
The Binh Dai Wind Power Plant project is located in the southeast sea area of Ben Tre province, Vietnam, about 55km-straight distance from the Ben Tre city centre. The whole project has a total capacity of 310 MW.
